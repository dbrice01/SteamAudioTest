Controls:

To move around:
-Use mouse for looking, WASD for movement

There are three Modes: Viewer (moving around scene), Boombox (moving boombox), and Scale (Scaling chamber). Mode can be toggled by pressing M key.

The axis used for movement/scaling in Boombox and Scale modes can be toggled between X,Y and Z by pressing the X key.
The W and S keys are used to scale/move up and down on an axis.
