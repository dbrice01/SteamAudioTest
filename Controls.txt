Controls:

To move around:
-Use mouse for looking, keyboard arrows for movement

Boombox:
-Hold B key down on keyboard for Boombox mode. In this mode hold X, Y or Z keys (individually) down (whilst still holding down B) and press arrow up or down keys to move the boombox on the selected axis

Chamber Scale:
- Hold S key down on keyboard for ChamberScale mode. Then hold X,Y or Z key and arrow up or arrow down to adjust the scale in the selected axis.
